Craterstation is a base of radio stations
Each plays a different style.
Internet radio 24/7

Since 2016, the music featured on most* of Craterstation
has been curated from independent artists only,
or independent labels who love sound and support original music.

You’ll hear all the real new sounds, DJ & underground styles,
plus some *nostalgia stations for that intense 50s-2000 listening.

The general styles are (but not limited to):  house, pop, bass, rave, techno, soft rock,
metal, future, drum-n-bass, synthwave, midtempo, club, vaporwave, alternative,
rock, vocal melodic, experimental, breakbeat, ambient, classical,
garage, trap, acid, dj sets, retro styles – and many more, obviously..

Enjoy the tracking-free, always on, non-commercial listening experience here.
 

 It’s for the music lovers and makers
